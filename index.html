Tasked

A simple to-do list for iPhone

<< | >>

Create, sort, mark, delete.

Tasked uses the built-in reminders functionality of iOS with a simple, colourful design to give you a back to basics approach for your to-do lists.

Features

  • Create new lists and new tasks.
  • Quickly strike things off your list, or use batch editing to mark several in one go.
  • Share important list items.
  • Read and edit each task's notes.
  • URL scheme support.
  • Your tasks are automatically synced to any devices with Reminders enabled - iPhone, iPad, or Mac.
